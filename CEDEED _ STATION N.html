Who are we

Who are we

We are a human potential development organization based in Nigeria

Human capital is the topic of the day and development is the business. Every Purpose driven nation that ever advanced into a first world nation or economy did that on the shoulders of their human capital before any other instrument is considered.

In nations like United State, Isreal, we can witness a powerful human capital force in action, from information technology, research and development, arm forces and the rest of it, human potentials cannot be overemphasized.

CEDEED (Station N) is a human potential development organization founded for the Nigerian people...

Our Core Activities

Our Core Activites

TALK ENGAGEMENTS

We provide a talk enegagement platform for inspirational speakers,thought leaders who are well experienced and as a matter of giving back to the society desires to share their stories of success, to an aspiring audeince striving to make a difference in Nigeria.

Ideas sharing has been a recent reoccuring events in our talk engagement programs since 2020, speakers are keen to see imparts and tremedous growths in their audience.

SKILL TRANSFER AND DEVELOPMENT

CEDEED provides skill transfer and development opportunities for all Nigerians who aspire to learn new skill to boost their performance, employability and competiveness in both the local and international settings.

The transferrable skills ranges from crafts to high technology, public management, business, security and safety, recreational and non profit skills etc.

COMMUNITY OUTREACH

Our monthly science, technology, engineering, arts and mathematics STEAM community outreaches are thrilling and fun filled. We designed this STEAM outreach to target both schools and other social instituition.

We understand the power of community engagement and we are using this tool to both spread the gospel of national transformation, as well as mobilize great minds from the audience who would be willign to venture into strong exploration to dig and develop the depths of their contents.

WORKPLACE MENTORSHIP

Solely for people who are training or building their ideas in CEDEED, we provide robust mentorship support in wide range of post- training projects. Our mentors also provide a range of help and advice to help you plan your career and make well-informed decisions along the way.
